I am a first-year Master's student of CS at the New York University , I received my B.S. in Mathematics from UT Austin in 2024, where I took some analysis and machine learning classes and did some bioinformatics research works.

My current research interests are Multimodal Learning, Vision-Language Model, and Embodied Learning. .

In particular, I am interested in how the multimodality can enable more human-like behaviors of machine learning models in terms of learning from few examples (few-shot learning ), memory mechanism (bring memory mechanism into multimodal models), or more generalizable decision making (RT series ) .

I had project experiences in both Vision and LLM .
